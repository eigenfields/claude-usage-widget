## Summary

This brand palette consists of four color families organized by tonal progression:

- **Slate**: A neutral dark family ranging from near-black to warm gray
- **Cloud**: A neutral gray family with subtle warmth across light to dark tones
- **Ivory**: A neutral light family ranging from soft off-white to pale cream
- **Warm**: An accent family with earthy, warm tones from terracotta to pale tan

Supporting colors include pure black and white for stark contrast, a blue focus color for interactive elements, and a red-toned error color for alerts and warnings.
